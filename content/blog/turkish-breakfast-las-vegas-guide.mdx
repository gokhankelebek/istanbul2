**Kahvalti** isn’t a single plate — it’s a **spread**. Think of it as the Turkish answer to brunch, built for grazing, sipping tea, and settling into the table.

## What you’ll typically find

- **Cheeses** and **olives**
- **Honey** and **jams** with fresh bread
- **Simit** (sesame-crusted ring bread)
- **Eggs** (often menemen-style, if you want something warm and savory)
- **Turkish tea**, poured and refilled throughout the meal

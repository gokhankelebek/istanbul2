We opened **Istanbul Mediterranean 2** at **505 Fremont Street** to give Las Vegas a true Turkish dining room in the heart of Downtown — with the same **100% Zabiha Halal** standards you expect from our family.

## What’s different from the Strip?

Fremont is built for slowing down: **full table service**, a larger dining room, **Kahvalti (Turkish breakfast)**, **lahmacun**, **pide**, and a bakery case — plus hours that stretch late for the neighborhood.

<Callout variant="hours">
  We’re open **daily from 10 AM**. Sunday–Wednesday until **2 AM**; Thursday–Saturday until **5 AM**.
</Callout>

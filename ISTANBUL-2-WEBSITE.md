## Our Story

### Bringing the Soul of Istanbul to Downtown

At Istanbul Mediterranean, we're more than just a restaurant — we're bringing the soul of Istanbul to the heart of Downtown Las Vegas. Our mission is to share centuries-old culinary traditions with a community that values authentic flavors and genuine hospitality.

Our flagship location at the Grand Bazaar Shops on the Strip has become a beloved destination for authentic Turkish street food. Now, with our second location in Fremont East, we're expanding our reach while staying true to our roots. The vibrant energy of Fremont East is the perfect home for our expanded vision.

Turkish hospitality — known as *Misafirperverlik* — is at the heart of everything we do. In our culture, the guest is "a guest of God," and we treat everyone who walks through our doors like family. This philosophy guides every interaction, every dish we prepare, and every moment we share with our community.

### Why Downtown Las Vegas?

"We chose Fremont East because of its incredible energy," owner Zane Atlas Muslu explains. "Downtown is undergoing a culinary renaissance, and we wanted to be part of a neighborhood that celebrates local grit, late-night culture, and authentic flavors. While the Strip is about the spectacle, Downtown is about the community — and that's where we want to grow."

### Chef Adem's Family Legacy

Behind every dish at Istanbul Mediterranean is Chef Adem, who carries on his family's legacy by preserving secret recipes passed down through generations. These aren't just recipes — they're stories, traditions, and a connection to Istanbul's rich culinary heritage.

Chef Adem ensures that every dish is prepared with care, using 100% Halal meats, fresh ingredients, and handcrafted techniques inspired by Istanbul's street food scene. From slow-roasted doner and gyro wraps to falafel and baklava, each item on our menu tells a story of Turkish culinary excellence.

Our commitment to authenticity means that when you dine with us, you're experiencing the same flavors, the same care, and the same hospitality that you would find in the streets of Istanbul. We're not just serving food — we're sharing a piece of our culture with every plate.

### A Real Turkish Welcome

As a family run by Turks, hospitality is in our DNA. We didn't just want to open another eatery; we wanted to create a space in Downtown where the community could gather. While our roots remain in tradition, our new space offers a fresh experience: cozy indoor seating where you can relax, escape the heat, and enjoy a meal that feels like home.

Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch or sitting down for a family dinner, we invite you to experience the authentic flavors and genuine hospitality that only a family business can provide.

> "We hope to provide a high-quality, authentic Halal option that stays open late — perfect for the hospitality workers, artists, and locals who make Downtown thrive. Each dish is prepared with care to inspire and delight everyone who walks through our doors." — Zane Atlas Muslu, Owner

## What Makes Istanbul 2 Different

### Full Table Service

Unlike our Strip location's quick-serve model, Fremont offers full table service. Dine in, sit down, and relax — or order delivery. A full-day destination from morning Kahvalti to late-night Pide.

### Expanded Menu

Celebrating the full spectrum of Turkish cuisine with breakfast, lunch, dinner, and late-night options. A complete Turkish dining experience.

### 100% Zabiha Halal

Same commitment to halal certification as our Strip location. All meats are hand-slaughtered according to strict Islamic dietary laws, with no pork or alcohol in any preparation. Our kitchen maintains strict separation from any non-halal items.

### Full-Day Destination

We aren't just a quick-service stop anymore; we are a full-day destination. Whether you want a rich breakfast to start your Saturday or a savory Pide after a night out on Fremont, we have something authentic waiting for you.

Vegan favorites, Falafel and Chi Kofte wraps, are also on the menu, ensuring everyone can enjoy authentic Turkish flavors.

## Expanded Menu Highlights

### Kahvalti (Turkish Breakfast)

A traditional Turkish breakfast spread featuring fresh cheeses, olives, honey, jams, eggs, and freshly baked breads. This is meant to be enjoyed with friends and family over endless cups of Turkish tea. Perfect for starting your Saturday or Sunday morning right.

*Exclusive to Fremont location — full breakfast experience.*

### Lahmacun (Turkish Pizza)

Authentic Lahmacun — paper-thin, crispy dough topped with a minced meat and vegetable herb blend. It's meant to be rolled up with fresh parsley and a squeeze of lemon for the authentic Turkish street food experience. New to Las Vegas!

### Pide (Turkish Boats)

Boat-shaped flatbreads baked in a high-heat oven, filled with everything from spicy sujuk (sausage) and melted cheese to seasoned vegetables. A hearty, satisfying meal perfect for any time of day.

### Turkish Pastries

Our bakery case is a major focal point, featuring handmade Baklava, flaky Borek, and other traditional Turkish pastries. Perfect for a morning treat or a late-night dessert after a night out on Fremont.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What are the Fremont Street location hours?**

Istanbul Mediterranean Restaurant-2 (Halal) is now open at 505 Fremont Street, Las Vegas, NV 89101. We're open daily from 10 AM (until 2 AM Sun-Wed, 5 AM Thu-Sat), serving our award-winning doner kebab and authentic Turkish flavors to Downtown Las Vegas.

**What makes the Fremont location different from the Strip location?**

The Fremont location features an expanded menu including Kahvalti (Turkish breakfast), Lahmacun (Turkish pizza), Pide (Turkish boats), and a full bakery case with Turkish pastries. It's designed as a full-day destination with a larger dining room, living green walls, cozy booths, and an open kitchen.

**Will the Fremont location have the same halal certification?**

Yes, absolutely. The Fremont location maintains the same 100% Zabiha Halal certification as our Strip location. All meats are hand-slaughtered according to strict Islamic dietary laws, with no pork or alcohol in any preparation.

**What is Kahvalti?**

Kahvalti is a traditional Turkish breakfast experience featuring a spread of fresh cheeses, olives, honey, jams, eggs, and freshly baked breads. It's meant to be enjoyed slowly with friends and family over endless cups of Turkish tea. This is exclusive to our Fremont location.
